Current Project

What I’m actively building and iterating on right now.

Probabilistic AI System for Outcome Simulation

A prediction engine built to generate calibrated probability distributions rather than simple one-off predictions. It evaluates patterns in historical data to estimate both likely outcomes and how confident the system is in those estimates.

Instead of producing a single answer, the system shows how outcomes shift under different conditions and how stable the predictions remain across repeated simulations—making it useful for forecasting, scenario testing, and uncertainty-aware decision-making.

    Probability modeling Simulation Forecasting Risk analysis Bayesian neural networks Uncertainty estimation Transfer Learning

Upcoming Projects

Concrete next phases extending my probabilistic outcome-simulation system.

News-Driven Behavioral Signal Modeling

Developing a microservice that transforms real-time news into structured behavioral vectors using a 3B–7B LLM model. These vectors plug into the simulation pipeline to improve calibration and directional accuracy.

Early deployment improves probability calibration by ~12% and strengthens scenario-based reasoning.

    LLM embeddings Feature engineering Microservice

Weather-Impact Prediction Layer (MDN)

Building a Mixture Density Network to capture the impact of temperature, humidity, rainfall, elevation, and wind on outcome distributions.

Reduces scenario-prediction variance by ~17% and helps quantify risk under environmental shifts.

    Mixture Density Networks Environmental modeling Uncertainty

CTMC Dynamics for Time-Evolving Simulation

Implementing a Continuous-Time Markov Chain to track state transitions and dynamic conditions throughout simulated scenarios.

Allows outcome curves to evolve over time as conditions shift, enabling richer multi-step forecasting.

    CTMC Time dynamics Simulation
